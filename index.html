A problem shared is Half Solved.

HalfSolved is a safe haven for problem sharing with people facing the same problem.

"An intuitive solution to a common problem that we all face, wrapped up in a single app!"

A platform where you can share your problems without fearing being judged.

HalfSolved is an application where the user logs in anonymously and has the priviledge of giving himself/herself a username. Once they are logged in they get access to a vast number of groups which are named according to the many problems people usually face in their day to day lives. What the user has to do is to look for the group that best describes what he is going through and go and view what people are saying and can also contribute. No one knows who you are in the different groups and so you are free to share anything and also give solutions to other people's problems.
